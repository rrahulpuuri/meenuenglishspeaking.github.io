Daily Vocabulary

Word of the Day Collection

Master one new word daily. Each word comes with meaning, usage, and a real-life example.

Articulate
Adjective / Verb
स्पष्ट रूप से बोलने में सक्षम; अपने विचार clearly express करना
"She was able to articulate her ideas clearly in the meeting."
Persist
Verb
किसी काम को बिना हार माने जारी रखना; डटे रहना
"He persisted in his efforts despite facing many rejections."
Elaborate
Verb / Adjective
किसी बात को विस्तार से समझाना; बहुत detailed होना
"Can you elaborate on your previous point for the team?"
Concern
Noun / Verb
चिंता, फ़िक्र; किसी बात से जुड़ा होना
"I have a concern about the project deadline."
Approach
Noun / Verb
किसी चीज़ तक पहुँचना; काम करने का तरीका
"Let's take a different approach to solve this problem."
Constructive
Adjective
जो सुधार में मदद करे; सकारात्मक और उपयोगी
"Please give constructive feedback so we can improve."
